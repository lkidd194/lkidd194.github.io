Vanilla

Vanilla is the most popular ice cream flavor. The flavor comes from vanillin - which is extract from vanilla beans. It was used by the French to flavor their ice cream. Thomas Jefferson discovered it in France and brought it to America. 

Combine it with other flavors
and fruits to get tasty treats!
